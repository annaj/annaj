My name is Anna Jacobsen and I show entrepreneurs how marketing automation can increase their conversions – especially through email automation.


Working At Drip/Leadpages

I joined Drip as employee #3, and a year later, Drip got acquired by Leadpages, which kept every Drip employee in their existing roles. The acquisition prompted a move from Drip HQ in sunny California to Leadpages HQ in Minnesota (brrr), and also allowed me to watch Drip become the fastest-growing marketing automation platform on the planet.
